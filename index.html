About Drift King

Drift King is an adrenaline-fueled racing experience that celebrates the art and skill of drifting. Unlike traditional racing games that focus solely on speed, Drift King rewards style, control, and precision as you master the perfect drift around challenging corners and tracks.

Game Features

  • Start with 30,000 in-game coins to immediately customize and upgrade your dream drift car
  • Explore six unique maps with different environments, from professional racetracks to winding countryside roads
  • Extensive garage with multiple cars, customization options, and performance upgrades
  • Multiplayer mode to challenge friends in drift competitions or casual races
  • Realistic physics engine that delivers an authentic drifting experience
  • Tune your vehicle's performance with adjustable camber, suspension height, and brake sensitivity

Controls

  • WASD or Arrow keys: Control your car's movement
  • M: Toggle rearview mirror
  • C: Change camera angle
  • R: Reset car position
  • U: Switch between KMH and MPH
  • I: Start/Stop engine
  • P: Toggle fullscreen mode

Garage & Customization

The garage is where your drift journey begins. Use your starting funds to:

  • Upgrade critical components like engine, turbo, gearbox, handling, and weight reduction
  • Customize your car's appearance with different paint colors and wheel options
  • Fine-tune performance with adjustable suspension settings for optimal drift angles
  • Balance your setup between power and control to match your personal drifting style

Multiplayer Experience

Create or join multiplayer rooms to connect with fellow drift enthusiasts. Challenge friends to drift competitions, race head-to-head, or simply cruise together while showing off your skills. The multiplayer mode transforms Drift King from a solo experience into a social playground where you can showcase your drifting mastery.

Tips & Tricks

Perfect Entry Speed

Finding the right entry speed for each corner is crucial. Too fast and you'll lose control; too slow and you won't drift effectively.

Counter-Steering

Master the art of counter-steering to maintain control during drifts. This technique involves turning your wheels in the opposite direction of your drift.

Chain Your Drifts

Link multiple drifts together to build combo multipliers and achieve higher scores. Practice transitioning smoothly between consecutive corners.

Track Memorization

Learn each track's layout to anticipate corners and prepare your drifts in advance. Knowledge of the track is as important as your driving skills.
